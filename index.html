Short Bio

I am currently an Assistant Researcher at the State Key Laboratory of Multimodal Artificial Intelligence Systems (MAIS), Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS). My research spans computer vision and artificial intelligence, with a focus on object detection/segmentation, zero/few-shot learning, cross-modal/multi-modal learning, AI4S, and remote sensing image understanding (e.g., satellite, UAV, and infrared data).

Before joining MAIS, I worked as a Senior Algorithm Expert at JD.com (2021–2022) and as a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Aerospace Information Research Institute (AIR), CAS (2018–2021).

I received my Ph.D. (2014-2017) from National Laboratory of Pattern Recognition, Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS), advised by Prof. Chunhong Pan.
